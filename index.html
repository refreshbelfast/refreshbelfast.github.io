Refresh with Mr. Bingo

The Conor Lecture Theatre, University of Ulster, Belfast., Doors 7pm.

We’re delighted to be teaming up with the Ulster Festival of Art and Design for a one-off special, ‘Refresh at the Ulster Festival’ event. We’ll be welcoming the inimitable Mr Bingo to Belfast, for an evening of informative and doubtless irreverent entertainment. As Mr Bingo puts it:

Some say he’s the ‘Master of Pens’. Some call him the ‘Justin Bieber of Drawing’. Some say he fucks about for a living.

Far from fucking about for a living, we admire his persistent work ethic, which has resulted in an enviable body of work, not least his first class Hate Mail, his hirsute Hair Portraits and his forthright insistence that

Join us for an evening lecture in the Conor Lecture Theatre, after which we will doubtless decamp to a local hostelry, or two, for much Mr Bingo merriment.

Refresh is where the web design community of Belfast gets together.

Every quarter, a group of smart, talented people gather to tell stories, share ideas, and drink a few beers at The Black Box.

Refresh brings together those working on the web in Belfast to learn from one another, promote better design, and build a more prosperous community. Established in Belfast by Andy McMillan, Refresh is now under the careful stewardship and direction of Tim Potter and Christopher Murphy.

Check out our YouTube Channel for previous Refresh talks or follow @refreshbelfast on Twitter for timely announcements.
